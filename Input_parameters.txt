###Uniform Experiment Settings###
###Example for FEI krios ###

1. Pixel information for your camera:
 NX=    NY=   QX=   QY=   !Number and Size (mm) of pixel

2. Overload range for your camera:
 OVERLOAD=           !default value dependent on the detector used

3. Resolution range for the 1st round:
 INCLUDE_RESOLUTION_RANGE=   

4. Direct beam position
 ORGX=   ORGY=  

5. Cameralength
 DETECTOR_DISTANCE=  

6. Oscillation range, degree per frame:
 OSCILLATION_RANGE=

7. Rotation axis, depending on microscope:
 ROTATION_AXIS=   !cos(rotationaxis) cos(axis-90)  !in XDS.INP

8. Wavelength, nm (200kev 0.02508, 300kev 0.01968):
 X-RAY_WAVELENGTH=       !used by IDXREF
